# CONTEXT layer

What the agent knows about your situation that no public model could ever know.

Three to five focused single-page files. Each covers one thing. Dated and fresh. Update when things change.

Suggested files:
- `stakeholders.md` — who reports to you, who you report to, key cross-functional partners
- `strategy.md` — what you are trying to achieve this year, the org's focus
- `principles.md` — how decisions get made, what you push back on, what you escalate
- `products.md` — what you ship and to whom
- `team.md` — the humans you work with day-to-day

Every time you find yourself re-explaining your situation to AI, that thing should be in a context file. Write it down. Move on.

Files in `.proposed/` are CORTEX-suggested updates from the nightly dream cycle. Approve them with `meridian context approve <file>`.
